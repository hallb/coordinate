# Risk Register

Formal risk identification is deferred to the solution phase. Key risk-adjacent inputs are already captured elsewhere:

- **Assumptions with identified risks**: See `08-constraints-and-assumptions.md` — each assumption includes a "Risk if Wrong" column and a validation plan.
- **Open research items**: See `07-scope.md` § Open Research Items (RI-001 through RI-004) — unresolved questions that may introduce risk depending on their outcome.
- **Technical constraints**: See `08-constraints-and-assumptions.md` § Constraints — particularly CON-002 (no insurer APIs) and CON-003 (2FA limitations).

Once the solution architecture is selected, this register should be populated with risks arising from implementation choices, technology dependencies, and operational concerns.
